Our Story

Leizel and Bill got married in San Diego, California on August 8, 2015. Three days later, they took a plane to Bangkok, Thailand to begin their honeymoon adventure. They traveled to three different countries: Thailand, Cambodia, and India. They hiked up a mountain through the sweltering jungles of Ang Thong near Koh Samui in the south of Thailand. In Cambodia, they visited the ruins of Angkor Wat and Angkor Thom. After returning to Bangkok, they saw the Royal Palace, Wat Phra Kaew, and the reclining Buddha. Then they ventured north to visit Chiang Mai where they rode elephants through the hillside and meditated with Buddhist monks in the ancient temples of the old city. Next, Leizel and Bill flew to the Indian subcontinent to journey around the Golden Triangle. They ventured through the crowded streets of New Dehli. They wandered around the Taj Mahal, Shah Jahan's everlasting memorial to his beloved wife. Finally, they traveled to Jaipur where they saw the pink city, the monkey temple of Galtaji, the floating palace of Jal Mahal, and the famous observatory of Jantar Mahar. After three weeks of traveling, Leizel and Bill headed home to New York City, having circumnavigated the world, opened their minds, and experienced a beautiful beginning to their marriage.
